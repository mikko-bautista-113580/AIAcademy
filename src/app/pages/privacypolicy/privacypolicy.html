Privacy Policy

How NPI AI Academy collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: {{ lastUpdated }}

Our Commitment to You

NPI AI Academy is an internal learning platform built to empower NPI professionals with AI knowledge and skills. We are committed to protecting the privacy of every team member who uses this platform. This policy explains how we handle information in alignment with our mission to democratize AI knowledge across all roles in our organization.

1 Information We Collect

As part of NPI AI Academy's mission to provide personalized, role-based AI education, we may collect the following information:

  • •Role Selection: The role you select (QA, BA, Developer, Technical Lead, or Management) is stored in your browser session to personalize your learning experience.
  • •Usage Data: Page visits and content interactions may be logged to improve the platform and tailor content to each role's needs.
  • •Authentication Data: Your organizational login credentials are managed entirely by Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD) and your organization's identity provider — we do not store passwords.
  • •Visitor Count: We track an anonymous, aggregate visitor count to measure platform engagement. Each browser session is counted once using a lightweight server-side counter. No personal information, user identity, or browsing behavior is recorded — only the total count of sessions is stored.

2 How We Use Your Information

NPI AI Academy uses information solely to fulfill its mission: helping every NPI team member grow their AI capabilities. Specifically, your information is used to:

  • •Personalize content, presentations, and demos based on your selected role.
  • •Improve the platform's content quality and relevance for different NPI roles.
  • •Ensure only authorized NPI team members can access internal AI learning materials.
  • •Track platform adoption to demonstrate AI's impact across the organization.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third party for marketing purposes.

3 Data Storage & Security

Protecting your data is a core responsibility aligned with NPI's commitment to building secure, high-quality software:

  • •Session Storage: Role preferences are stored locally in your browser session and are cleared when you close your browser. No role data is sent to external servers.
  • •Visitor Counter Storage: The aggregate visitor count is stored using Netlify Blobs, a secure server-side key-value store provided by our hosting platform. Only a single numeric value (the total session count) is stored — no user identifiers, IP addresses, or personal data are recorded or associated with this counter.
  • •Hosting: The platform is hosted on Netlify with Azure AD authentication. Content is accessible only to authenticated NPI team members.
  • •No External Tracking: NPI AI Academy does not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or external cookies. The visitor counter is an internal, first-party mechanism that stores no personally identifiable information.

4 Your Rights

As an NPI team member, you have the right to:

  • •Switch or reset your role at any time from the role selection screen.
  • •Request information about what data (if any) has been collected about your usage.
  • •Request deletion of any identifiable usage data associated with your account.

To exercise these rights, please contact the NPI AI Academy team through the internal communication channels.

5 Changes to This Policy

As NPI AI Academy grows and evolves its AI learning programs, this Privacy Policy may be updated. Any significant changes will be communicated to team members through internal channels. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how NPI AI Academy handles your data, reach out to the AI Academy team through NPI's internal communication channels.
